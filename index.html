About Us

Our token and NFT collection focuses on building a strong community and taking things back to where the phenomenon of DEFI tokens really began. Diamond Doge NFT holders represent the true core of the crypto currency community. We have the strength and spirit to move markets! As an NFT holder you get access to our special private community. Our private community is one of the best in the space - period!

The Diamond Doge NFT Collection is a set of 777 unique and rare NFT's that exist on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT also has its own perk of earning you passive income in $DOGE, while our DDOGE token holders can benefit from our passive staking and growth tokenomics.

Diamond Doge is run by and experienced and trusted developer that has already launched many successful projects in the past. Along his army of loyal Diamond Doge holders, he plans to show the world the true power of a tight-knit community!

Our ROADMAP

As well as rewarding our NFT & token holders, we intend to build a suite of tools aimed at making Dogecoin more accessible from other networks.

We will build an active bridge that will allow a Dogecoin network user to swap their Dogecoin with the native coins of multiple chains! We also intend to build a private launchpad platform. Our NFT and token holders will get early access to projects launching through our launchpad.
